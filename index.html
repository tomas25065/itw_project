Who am i ?

Musician, Producer, Dj, Graphic Designer, Street-artist and recently videographer, discover the `` EFIX `` artist that I am, what are my artistic approaches, my background, and my future projects.

My project

Traveling around the world solo, flying over more than 45 countries, meeting people living outside modern society, discovering their cultures, customs and music with the aim of composing my first album...

Street art

In parallel with the music, I develop a plastic work , which I exhibit in the streets of the world, captivating the attention of people who are not typical museum visitors and thus offers a mix between street-art and graphics , where I plays with references from pop culture and our childhood, without losing the dreamy tone that characterizes me in all my artistic work. My approach in street-art is to adapt to architecture or urban elements in order to divert them in order to make passers-by smile. Basically a graphic designer, and after studying at an art school (ESMA) , I learned to master photo / video editing software in order to be able to put my lucid dreams into images* (as weird as they are) and thus, in addition to my street arts, create digital works tinged with humor and poetry on the border between the real and the imaginary world.

My work

Music

Producer/Dj for 10 years now, my music has been played all over the world and counts millions of views. The atmosphere I create and the vibe I try to give off are a mix... between dreamy and groovy, inspired by Reggae, Soul, Indie. My goal is to mix so-called “real” instruments with house/techno. My evolution and my concerts around the planet have given me an open mind and a vision of the world which have allowed me to develop my musical culture towards world music. And at the same time to create my own artistic approach: sampler (record) using my microphones and my mobile studio the ambient elements or artists that I meet on my way to put them in my music in order to create works unique with a story.

Travel

Passionate about art and seeing the world as an inexhaustible source of inspiration , but above all as a real school of life, intense and wonderful. In recent years my concerts in the 4 corners of the planet have led me to travel a lot and thus live each trip as a new experience. Living from day to day, enjoying every moment while facing loneliness, fear of the unknown, and nostalgia… in short, all these parameters that make us feel much more ALIVE and make us know ourselves better. During these last 6 years, I have been able to record hundreds of artists , and thus make exceptional encounters (tribes / street artists, etc.) full of humility and love which have led me to want to create the project: “Borders Lost“ revolved around my passions.

Discover the project

BORDERS LOST

Going around the world alone, flying over more than 45 countries, meeting traditional peoples and societies living apart from modern society, discovering their cultures , customs and their music with the aim of composing an album and donate part of the funds through my humanitarian association in order to preserve their culture. While proposing a project in line with the problems of society.
